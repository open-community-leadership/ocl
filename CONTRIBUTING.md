# Contributing

Thank you for helping improve Open Community Leadership.

This project welcomes contributions from community organisers, maintainers, volunteers, facilitators, and participants from all open source ecosystems.

## Ways to contribute

- Suggest improvements to modules
- Add examples from your community
- Translate resources
- Share case studies
- Improve templates and worksheets
- Report confusing sections
- Propose new workshop activities

## Contribution principles

- Keep the language practical and accessible.
- Prefer examples over theory.
- Avoid corporate management jargon where plain language works better.
- Respect that communities differ by region, language, culture, size, and resources.
- Do not present one community model as universal.

## Case studies

When contributing a case study, please avoid sharing private conflict details, identifiable personal information, or sensitive internal community matters without consent.
