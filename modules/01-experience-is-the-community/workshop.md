# OCL Workshops

Workshops are facilitated sessions that bring OCL modules to life in a group setting.

Each workshop is self-contained: everything needed to participate in or facilitate it is in the workshop folder. All workshops are free to run, adapt, and share under CC BY 4.0.

Before facilitating a workshop, it is strongly recommended to attend it once as a participant.
